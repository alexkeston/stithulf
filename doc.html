What is Stithulf

Stithulf is a technology company that specializes in innovating and building creative banking applications, as well as economic and financial instruments, aimed at improving, scaling, and stabilizing the global economy.

Our company's objective is to establish a connection between traditional banking practices and the world of cryptocurrency.
